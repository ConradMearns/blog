> "A breadboard is a construction base for prototyping of electronics.
> Originally the word referred to a literal bread board, a polished piece of wood used for slicing bread.
> In the 1970s the solderless breadboard (a.k.a. plugboard, a terminal array board) became available and nowadays the term "breadboard" is commonly used to refer to these." - Wikipedia
